Drug Response Predictions Powered By Genetic Diversity

Coral improves drug development and deployment by utilizing genomic data from patients to determine how they will respond to a drug before they get treated.

This will result in more more efficient clinical trials and healthcare delivery.

How it works

We measure and reduce variability in drug response

Target Variability

Coral determines how likely it is that cells within a patient will respond to a drug based on their genetic profile.

Response Variability

Coral then performs experiments to measure variability across patients and finds ways to reduce that variation.

Recommendations

Finally, Coral recommends treatment strategies.
