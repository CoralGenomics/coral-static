Harnessing Genetic Data To Optimize Your Drug Development Process.

Coral improves the clinical trial process by utilizing genomic data from patients to determine how they will respond to a drug before they get treated.

This will result in more successful clinical trials by letting your team know what type patients should be recruited based on their genetic profile.

How it works

We determine variability in response for your compound

Target Variability

Coral determines how likely is it that your compounds response will be variable across patient populations and cell types based on their genetic profile.

Response Variability

Coral then screens your compound to find exactly which genes cause the drug response to be variable in
patients and cells.

Recommendations

Finally, Coral helps you determine which patients should be selected for your clinical trials based on whether their genomic profile indicates they are likely to respond to the treatment.
